Ilocos Norte Accessibility Finder

Many tourists with mobility or accessibility needs struggle when planning trips because essential information—like wheelchair-friendly paths, low-walking routes, shaded areas, or braille-guided spaces—is often missing from traditional travel guides. This lack of inclusive travel resources makes it difficult for visitors to confidently choose destinations that match their needs.

The Ilocos Norte Accessibility Finder was created to bridge that gap. This simple and user-friendly platform helps travelers discover tourist spots tailored to their accessibility requirements. Whether you need wheelchair-accessible locations, minimal walking, sun-protected areas, or other accommodations, the tool provides meaningful suggestions to ensure a safer, more comfortable, and more enjoyable experience exploring Ilocos Norte.
